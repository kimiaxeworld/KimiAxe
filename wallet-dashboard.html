Wallet Terms & Conditions

1. Wallet Services

AxeB2B.wallet provides a prepaid digital wallet for use across KimiAxe platforms. The wallet is not a banking product and does not earn interest.

2. KYC Requirement

Wallets above ₹10,000 balance or monthly transactions above ₹50,000 require KYC verification (Aadhaar + PAN).

3. Limits

Non-KYC: ₹10,000 max balance, ₹5,000/day transactions. KYC verified: ₹1,00,000 max balance, ₹50,000/day transactions.

4. Prohibited Use

Wallets may not be used for money laundering, illegal transactions, or circumventing financial regulations.

5. Closure

Accounts suspected of fraud are frozen pending investigation. Balance is returned after verification.

eSIM Terms & Conditions

1. eSIM Activation

eSIM plans are activated instantly via QR code. Once activated, plans are non-refundable as data consumption begins immediately.

2. Device Compatibility

You are responsible for checking device compatibility before purchase. Refunds are not available for incompatible devices.

3. Coverage

Coverage quality depends on local network partners. AxeB2B.wallet is not liable for network outages by third-party carriers.

4. Validity

eSIM plan validity starts from activation date. Unused data after expiry is forfeited.

5. Fair Use

Unlimited plans are subject to fair use. Speeds may be reduced after 50GB usage per month.
